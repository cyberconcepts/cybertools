Collect Version Information from Different Packages
===================================================

$Id$

  >>> from cybertools.util.version import versions

  >>> versions.add()

  >>> v = versions.get('cybertools.util.version')
  >>> v
  cybertools.util.version 0.4-3014
  >>> print v
  0.4-3014
  >>> v.short
  '0.4'

  >>> v = versions.get('foo')
  >>> v
  foo 0.0-3014
  >>> print v
  0.0-3014
